# Hero's Essay Blueprint - Curriculum Engine

## 7. Core Writing Tracks & Prompts

### 7.1. Track 1: The Hero's Journey Track
*   **Phase 1A: The Heart** - Q1 (Core experience), Q2 (Transformation formula), Q2.5 (Essential Belief), Q3 (Magic Elixir / Application).
*   **Phase 1B: The Spine** - Q4 (What was at risk), Q5 (Complication/Grounded Climax), Q6 (Winning action), Q7 (Ordinary vs Special World).
*   **Phase 2: The Arc** - Q8 (Inciting Incident), Q9 (Hesitation/Doubt), Q10 (Commitment/Crossing Threshold).

### 7.2. Track 2: Different But Truthful Track
1.  **The Silent Admission:** Break down applicant facade with absolute vulnerability.
2.  **The Tipping Point:** A specific, low-key confrontation.
3.  **The Unseen Labor:** Micro-action in the shadows.
4.  **The Messy Spectrum Reflection:** Analysis of continuous growth.
5.  **The Quiet Integration:** College promise.

### 7.3. Track 3: Intellectual Journey Track
1.  **The Intellectual Spark:** Specific puzzle/obsession.
2.  **The Evolving Pursuit:** Complicating theory (cognitive dissonance) or Deepening complexity.
3.  **The Synthesizing Pivot:** Logical analysis resolving the tension.
4.  **The Intellectual Paradigm Shift:** New mental model/truth.
5.  **The Laboratory Promise:** Scholarly integration at the university.

## 9. Anti-Patterns & Clichés
*   **The Resume Repeat:** Listing accomplishments instead of telling a story.
*   **The "Voluntourism" Savior:** Hollow virtue regarding developing nations.
*   **The Sports Highlight Reel:** Focusing on physical victory rather than internal challenge.
*   **The Trauma Dump:** Over-rotating on pain rather than focusing 70% on recovery and agency.
*   **The Passive Protagonist:** Letting luck or parents solve the problem.

## 10. Cinematic Prose Tools
*   **The Zoom Lens:** Slowing down to isolate sensory details at the climax.
*   **The Montage:** Speeding up to string together eras under a common theme.

## 11. Narrative Beat Comparisons
Examples spanning student essays (Camp Counselor, Back-yard Vaulting, Torque Wrench) to cinematic equivalents (Harry Potter, Legally Blonde).

## 12. Appendix: Pixar’s 22 Rules of Storytelling
Preserves the original 22 rules as established by Pixar Animation Studios, emphasizing stakes, character challenge, and emotional honesty over complex worldbuilding.
